Call for papers

Nepal Statistical Society is pleased to invite Academicians, Researchers, Data Scientists, Statisticians, Professionals, and Students to NCETASD-2025, which is taking place on May 29-30, 2025 (15-16 Jestha, 2082). This conference brings us together to explore the latest advancements in Statistics and Data Science, providing a valuable platform to present research, share knowledge, and engage with thought leaders shaping the future of these fields. Join us for insightful keynote addresses, panel discussions, technical sessions, and networking opportunities on contemporary themes such as public health, finance, AI, machine learning, environmental analytics, governance, and more.

The theme for this year's conference is Application of Statistics and Data Science.

Host Organization: Orchid International College

Orchid International College located at Bijay Chowk Gaushala, Kathmandu, Nepal, is a reputed institution offering undergraduate and graduate programs in IT, management, and business studies, fostering academic excellence and professional growth.
