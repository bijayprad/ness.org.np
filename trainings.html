One Day R Workshop for Beginners

Nepal Statistical Society (NeSS) is organizing a One-Day R Workshop for Beginners. This workshop is designed for individuals interested in learning the fundamentals of R programming for data analysis. It is ideal for students, researchers, and professionals who have basic computer literacy and foundational statistical knowledge. The session will cover essential R concepts, including data manipulation, visualization, and basic statistical techniques, helping participants develop practical skills for real-world data analysis. This hands-on workshop will be a great opportunity to enhance your analytical capabilities with R. Interested participants are encouraged to contact us for registration and further details. Don't miss this chance to build your data analysis skills with R!
